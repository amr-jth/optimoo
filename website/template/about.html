About Optimoo

Our Mission

Optimoo is dedicated to advancing livestock farming through innovative technologies. Our goal is to provide farmers with precise, efficient, and sustainable feeding solutions for their cattle.

About the Project

"Feed Optimisation Using Precision Livestock Farming (Optimoo)" is a project by Amarjith M A, Ameen Ali P M, Mekha Rosh, and Sriniketh R, under the Computer Science and Engineering Department at TKM College of Engineering. Leveraging machine learning and data analytics, the platform provides customized feeding patterns for cattle, considering factors like climate, season, and individual health.

By ensuring reliability, specificity, and scientific accuracy, Optimoo empowers farmers to adopt smarter, more sustainable feeding practices.

Why Choose Us?

  • Reliable: Advanced algorithms ensure accurate feeding patterns.
  • Specific: Tailored recommendations for individual cattle.
  • Scientific: Based on proven research and validated datasets.

Meet the Team

Our team of passionate engineers—Amarjith M A, Ameen Ali P M, Mekha Rosh, and Sriniketh R—collaborated to create a platform that integrates technology and agriculture, aiming to enhance productivity and sustainability.
